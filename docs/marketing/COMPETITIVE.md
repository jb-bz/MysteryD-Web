## MysteryD Differentiation Points

1. **AI-Native Development** — Leverage agent/AI tooling for faster development cycles than traditional agencies
2. **Developer-First Philosophy** — Deep technical expertise, not just "store setup" but actual platform extensibility
3. **Iterative Delivery** — Agent-powered development enables rapid iteration vs. traditional project milestones
4. **Cost Efficiency** — AI-assisted development allows competitive pricing without sacrificing quality
