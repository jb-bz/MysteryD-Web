## Negative Keywords

Add these to prevent irrelevant clicks:

- free
- cheap
- job
- career
- intern

- wordpress
- woocommerce
- magento

---

## Bidding Strategy

**Initial:** Manual CPC with max bid of $50 (conservative while testing)

**After 30 days with data:**
- If CPA < $250: Switch to Target CPA
- If conversion rate > 3%: Increase bids on top-performing keywords
- If CPL > $400: Pause keywords or revise ad copy

---

## Geographic Targeting

**Primary:** United States, Canada, UK (English-speaking markets)

**Exclude:**
- Countries with high fraud rates (work with Google Ads support to identify)
- Any geographic area where we cannot legally do business

---

## Ad Schedule

**Initial:** Run 24/7 for first 30 days to gather data

**After 30 days:** Analyze performance by day/hour and adjust schedule accordingly.

## 30-Day Evaluation Criteria

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Cost per MQL | < $250 | Pause or revise |
| Conversion rate | > 2% | Test new ad copy |
| Click-through rate | > 3% | Improve ad relevance |
| Impressions | > 10,000/month | Increase bids or expand keywords |
