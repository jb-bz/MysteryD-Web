## ICP Definitions

### Primary ICP: Shopify Growth-Stage Merchant

**Who:**
- Founder or Head of E-commerce at a Shopify Plus merchant
- Doing $500K - $10M ARR
- 2-20 person team, no large dev staff
- Sells direct-to-consumer (DTC), likely multiple traffic channels

**Pain:**
- Overpaying for bloated all-in-one apps that create integration complexity
- Can not get customization they need from app store plugins
- Vendors treat them like a number, not a partner

**Behavior:**
- Actively researching "Shopify app development" or "custom Shopify apps"
- Frustrated with template solutions
- Willing to pay premium for focused solution that actually solves their problem

**Buying trigger:**
- A/B test failure they can not solve with existing tools
- Checkout optimization project
- Post-purchase experience they can not customize in Shopify natively

### Secondary ICP: Developer / Indie Hacker

**Who:**
- Technical founder building on Shopify
- Solo or 1-2 person team
- Building a SaaS that extends Shopify
- Probably using Shopify Dev Store for their own brand

**Pain:**
- Needs to move fast, can not wait 8 weeks for agency quotes
- Wants developer-first tooling and documentation
- Needs transparent pricing, no "contact sales" gate

**Behavior:**
- Active in developer communities (Hacker News, Reddit r/shopify, Shopify Community forums)
- Evaluating based on code quality, open-source, technical docs
- Will recommend to their network if they have a good experience

**Buying trigger:**
- Looking for a partner who can move at their speed
- Needs a technical capability they can not build themselves
- Values self-serve over sales calls

## Qualified Lead Criteria

### Marketing Qualified Lead (MQL)

**Merchant ICP:**
- visited /apps or /blog on mysteryd.com
- Downloaded code sample, signed up for waitlist, or requested early access
- Company: Shopify Plus merchant, 50-500 employees
- Role: Founder, Head of E-commerce, CTO, or similar decision-maker

**Developer ICP:**
- Starred mysteryd GitHub repo OR joined Discord/community
- Downloaded technical documentation or API reference
- Role: Technical founder, indie developer, Shopify partner

### Sales Qualified Lead (SQL)

**Both ICPs:**
- Booked a discovery call via website or outreach
- Responded to cold outreach with positive intent signal
- Completed product-led trial or demo request
- Budget: $500+ monthly for ongoing app partnership OR $5K+ for custom development project

### When to Self-Serve vs. Sales Engage

| Scenario | Route |
|----------|-------|
| Developer evaluating open-source tool | Self-serve, GitHub, Discord |
| Merchant researching Shopify app options | Self-serve, blog content, waitlist |
| Merchant with specific custom app need | Sales-assisted evaluation |
| Enterprise-level customization request | Sales, likely >$20K project |
| Technical integration question | Developer relations, Discord/email |

## Messaging Framework

### Brand Positioning

**Thesis:** The Shopify app ecosystem is dominated by bloated all-in-one suites and cheap plugins that create new problems while solving old ones. MysteryD builds focused tools that do exactly what they say, backed by humans who answer.

**Tagline:** "No black boxes. Just better apps."

### Core Messaging Pillars

| Pillar | For Whom | What We Say | What It Means |
|--------|----------|-------------|---------------|
| **Focused** | Merchants | "One problem. Solved." | We build one thing and do it well. No feature bloat. |
| **Honest** | Merchants + Developers | "No black boxes." | Transparent pricing, clear scope, no surprise bills. |
| **Fast** | Developers + Indie Hackers | "Ship in days, not sprints." | AI-assisted development means faster delivery than traditional agencies. |
| **Partner** | Growth Merchants | "Built with you." | We embed with your team, not just deliver code. |

### Voice and Tone

- **Direct:** Say what we mean. No marketing fluff.
- **Confident:** We know our craft. Not arrogant, just certain.
- **Grounded:** We ship real things. Show the work, not just the pitch.
- **Dry-witted:** We have a sense of humor but do not force it.

### Key Messages by ICP

**To Growth-Stage Merchant (Primary):**
> "Stop paying for apps that do 20 things badly. We build exactly what you need, tell you what it will cost upfront, and answer when you call."

**To Developer / Indie Hacker:**
> "Need to ship a Shopify integration fast? We write clean code, open-source our work, and do not gate the docs behind a contact form."
